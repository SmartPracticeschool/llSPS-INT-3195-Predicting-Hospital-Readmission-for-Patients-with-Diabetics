Hospital Readmission For Patients With Diabetics

Result


Chance of readmission : 64%







While the overall 30-day readmission rate of hospitalized patients is 8.5-13.5% , the 30-day readmission rate of diabetics patients is 14.4-22.7%

A hospital readmission is an episode when a patient who had been discharged from a hospital is admitted again within a specified time interval. Readmission rates had increasingly been used as an outcome measure in health services
